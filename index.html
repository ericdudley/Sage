General ways to save money

  • Save your change in a jar.

    Due to inflation, the value of change has decreased, and most people try to avoid dealing with it; however, over time, a sizable amount of money can be saved. It builds up faster than you might think!

  • Pay yourself before you pay anybody else!

    Make sure you put your own savings account as your most important payee. If you take a portion of your paycheck and transfer straight to savings before spending any of it, you will end up saving a lot more money.

  • Keep track of what you buy.

    Think about what you buy that you don't really need. You do not need a nail salon's supply of nail polish, nor do you need every type of chewing gum in your car; it is these little extra costs that add up and steal your money. Whenever you are about to go out to a store or restaurant think about what you need to buy, and do not let yourself buy anything else.

  • Sacrifice

    Nobody likes giving up luxuries, but in order to save money, some of the finer things in life have to be given up. Rather than buying the best of a certain product with all the bells and whistles, try taking a step down and buying something more sensible.
